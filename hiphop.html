About

Hip Hop is in an social media platform . Where Users can share/upload videos or images and can add stories.Hip Hop was initiated while Tik Tok ban officially announced by indian government to give the treat to the tik tok users and to explore the hidden talents of user to the world

Technologies Used

App launched in playstore and hosted at AWS .App made by Flutter , Django and Apps Script .SQlite to store and manipulate the data .Firebase used for push notification , In-App-Update and to push dynamic ads manually from firebase.
